What to Expect

From your first hello to your finished smile, here is how it works.

01 Request your free consult +−
Fill out a quick form or give us a call. Our team reaches out to find a time that works for you, at whichever office is closest. No referral needed.
02 Meet Dr. Williams +−
Your first visit is a friendly, no-pressure look at your smile. Dr. Williams examines teeth, bite, and jaw growth, and for kids he also considers breathing and airway as part of the whole picture.
03 Get a clear plan +−
You leave with plain-language options, whether that's braces, clear aligners like Invisalign, or simply "let's check again in six months." We help with insurance questions and flexible payment plans.
04 Start treatment +−
Digital scans and 3D imaging make visits comfortable and precise. You see the same trusted doctor every time, and appointments are planned around school and work.
05 Keep your smile for life +−
When treatment wraps up, retainers and easy follow-up visits help protect the result. Many families stay with us for every sibling's smile.
