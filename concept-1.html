Airway-Focused Orthodontics

Straight teeth are only part of the story. Dr. Williams also looks at how your child breathes, sleeps, and grows. An airway-focused approach considers the jaw, palate, and airway together, and treatment is designed to support healthy breathing and better sleep alongside a confident smile.

Wondering about snoring, mouth breathing, or restless sleep? Bring it up at your free consult. We will take a look together and explain what we see in plain language.
